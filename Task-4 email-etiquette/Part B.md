**The "Do's" of Responsible Social Media**

 \* Do set your personal accounts to private and regularly audit who is following you.  
 \* Do build a professional presence on LinkedIn to connect with professors and alumni.  
 \* Do think about your future career; assume every post is permanent and searchable.  
 \* Do use your platform to support others, share campus events, and highlight your achievements.  
 \* Do verify the credibility of news or campus rumors before you hit the "share" button.

**The "Don'ts" of Responsible Social Media**

 \* Don't post photos or videos involving illegal substances, underage drinking, or academic dishonesty.  
 \* Don't engage in online arguments or cyberbullying; keep disagreements private and respectful.  
 \* Don't share sensitive info, such as your student ID number, home address, or current class schedule.  
 \* Don't badmouth your university, professors, or internships; it reflects poorly on your character.  
 \* Don't post while highly emotional; if you're angry or upset, step away from the screen for a few hours.
